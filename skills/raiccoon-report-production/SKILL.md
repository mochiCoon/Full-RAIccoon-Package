# RAIccoon report production

Use this skill when turning research tasks, sandbox evidence, or threat-intel notes into a final report.

Default sections:
- Executive summary
- Key findings
- Evidence and analysis
- Impact and risk
- Recommendations
- Indicators or detections when applicable
- Source notes

Keep templates organization-neutral until the adopter adds their own branding.
